Welcome to your new setup, guided by Orlo the Whale!

## Who is Orlo?
Orlo is your friendly whale who helps you automate and beautify your development environment. With a wave of his fin, Orlo installs all the tools you need and makes your macos  development experience delightful.

## 'The less you do' 
- Automated installation of essential CLI tools, editors, and utilities
- Optional setup for advanced tools like LazyVim, LM Studio, Ollama, and Claude Code
- Friendly prompts and clear instructions
- Final cleanup and optimization

## How to Use
1. Make the build script executable:
   ```sh
   chmod +x build.sh
   ```
2. Run the script:
   ```sh
   ./build.sh
   ```
3. Follow Orlo's prompts to customize your setup.

## Why Orlo?
Orlo brings personality and ease to your setup process, making it less intimidating and more enjoyable. Plus, you get a beautiful whale in your terminal!
